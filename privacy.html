Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Glue Stick! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our Discord bot, Glue Stick. By using Glue Stick, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Data

When you use Glue Stick, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Discord User ID: A unique identifier assigned to you by Discord.
  • Discord Username: Your public username and discriminator (e.g., Username#1234).
  • Server Information: Details about the server where Glue Stick is being used, including server ID and server name.
  • Message Content: Only the content of messages that specifically interact with Glue Stick's commands. Glue Stick does not log or store any other messages sent within a server.
  • Sticked Messages: Content and metadata of messages that are sticked by the bot, including timestamps and the user who initiated the stick.

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect information on how Glue Stick is accessed and used, including:

  • Command Usage: Logs of the commands executed within the server.
  • Interactions: Details of user interactions with Glue Stick, such as command success or failure rates.

3. How We Use the Information

3.1 Provide and Maintain the Service

  • Command Execution: We use the collected data to execute and respond to commands within Discord servers.
  • Sticky Message Management: We store sticked messages and manage their visibility within the server.

3.2 Improve the Service

  • Performance Analysis: We analyze usage patterns to improve the functionality and efficiency of Glue Stick.
  • Bug Fixes: We use data to diagnose and fix any technical issues that arise.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

Glue Stick does not share your data with third-party services except for the following:

  • Hosting Services: We use third-party hosting providers to operate and maintain Glue Stick.
  • Data Storage: We use SQLite databases to store data related to Glue Stick's functionality. These databases are hosted on secure servers.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

5. Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as it is necessary to provide the services offered by Glue Stick. Specifically:

  • Sticked Messages: Stored until automatically cleared according to the deletion protocol.
  • Command Logs: Retained for a period necessary to analyze performance and address any issues.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
